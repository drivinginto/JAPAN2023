Japanese Perspectives on Our Shared Energy Future

This website documents the results of Rebecca Rice's 2021 Eli Lilly Teacher Creativity Grant.

Find Out More

Cycling, hiking, and 国際交流 (international exchange) in rural Japan.

In the summer of 2023, I revisited Japan, where I had originally begun my teaching adventure in middle school education ten years ago. I participated in a two-week long Japanese conversation course in Kyoto, biked the countryside of Hokkaido to gain Japanese perspectives on our shared energy future, and created two projects for our 8th grade electric grid & climate change unit.
